Utah and Nevada

Amtrak from Granby to San Francisco

A marathon 33 hour train ride took us from Granby (CO), through Utah and Nevada to San Francisco, with scenery ranging from the magnificent Rocky Mountains to pine forests, valleys, arid plains, desert, sand dunes and the night lights of Salt Lake City. A couple of brief stops along the way to catch some fresh air and stretch our legs along station platforms, we stepped foot in Green River (UT), Reno (NV) and Roseville (CA) (which turned out to be not enough stretching of the legs). Communal meals in the dining car, stunning views, spending some time in the scenery car, and then seeing the most puzzling site of ships in the desert, which we then realized was actually San Francisco Bay... with land so flat we couldn't see the water that the ships were floating on!
